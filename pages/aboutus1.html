About Ebastaa.online

With change and need of the Industry and corporate world, the stakeholders including Institutions are bound to bring quality in higher education with setting up CoEs ( centre of Excellence), IQAC (Internal Quality Assurance cell) for which eBastaa.online can work as a bridge between the Institutions and edu tech service providers to make capacity building of an Institution in line with New Education Policy of India. The internships/trainings are the most significant component of professional learners for which eBastaa.online would provide solutions.

Unlike other online platforms, the platform offered by this startup will assist institutes in the area of accreditation, quality based certifications (i.e. ISO etc), conducting electrical energy auditing and green auditing. It will also assist faculty members for uploading their E- contents to empower themselves in their own field of expertise. It will also support students for online competitions, e-learning modules mapped with OBE, etc. Get To Know Us eBastaa.online is broadening its horizon expeditiously. Vision

To emanate a premier and sustainable partner for holistic growth of Institutions to make them to serve society for development of nation by nurturing them with Edge Cutting Technologies ,Life skills, Ethics with balancing Physical and Mental Health.



Mission

  • To bring the Edu-Tech solutions as an indigenous online platform for Higher Education Institutions through which equality, accessibility, quality and affordable teaching learning be made for employability skills enhancement.

  • To act as a bridge between the Institutions and Edu Tech service providers to make barrier free environment of learning for capacity building of an Institution through empowering their faculty and staff members.

  • To provide exposure to the professional learners as Global Citizen through internships and trainings.

  • To emphasis more on Research Index with Entrepreneur mind set by maintaining excellent Happiness Index of Institutions.
